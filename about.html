Who We Are

AN AMERICAN COMPANY
WITH REGIONAL ROOTS

StrataFlow Energy Group is a US-incorporated procurement company purpose-built to serve as the official procurement hub for materials, machinery, and services required by Latin America's energy sector.

We operate as part of a group corporate structure alongside VENETECA, an established oil services company with over a decade of active field operations in the region's major oil producing areas. This two-entity model gives clients the best of both worlds: American corporate rigor for procurement and contracting, and local field presence for execution.

Oil and gas operators in the region increasingly need to route procurement through US-based entities to access American technology, capital equipment, and services. StrataFlow provides exactly that: a compliant, legally structured, and operationally capable American partner ready to deliver at scale.
